Welcome to Dragon Legends

What are Dragon Legends?

Bringing alive Dragons you always wanted to see

Dragon Legends is an upcoming multiplayer fighting game. We are bringing fighting genre to Metaverse and mythical dragons to the modern world. Choose one of 5 Dragon egg types and hatch your own Dragon. Each Dragon comes with a set of accessories. Yes, you read it right. Some of them are tatooed, some of them have piercings and much, much more.

Dragon Legends NFT

Every ingame character is represented by a NFT and every Dragon is unique.

For the first time ever, You can earn money by playing video games from your home. Trade with other players, wager your Dragon tokens, rent out accessories to make money.

How to mint

Check the price

NFT Prices fluctuate depending on the price of ETH. Make sure to check the price regularly.

Decide when to buy

Once the NFT price reaches your desired price, you can buy it.

Mint when ready

Each transaction is limited to 30 NFTs, but there is no max limit per wallet.
